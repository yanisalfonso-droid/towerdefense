// FIELD MANUAL — v4

Objectif

Survive 30 waves. You have only 3 lives.
Each wave ends with a Mini-Boss.
Massive Bosses every 10 waves.
Waves 22-30 are extremely hard.

⚠ Boss Danger

Some bosses destroy towers in their path!
Watch for the warning and reposition.

🧱 Obstacles

2 random obstacle zones block tower placement.
Only flying-range towers can be placed behind them.
Actually no tower can go ON obstacles.

Flying Enemies ✈

Ground towers cannot hit flying units.
✈ CAPIBARA AA is anti-air only (max 4).
Tung Tung, Brr Brr & Tripi Tropi also hit air.

Mid-Wave Events

Random events can happen during a wave:
⚡ Speed burst · 🛡 Enemy shields · 💰 Bonus gold
🌫 Ghost storm · 🔥 Rage wave · ✈ Air raid
